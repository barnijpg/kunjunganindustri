UNIKOM

first ACTIVITY

After leaving PT Chlorine, all the 10th grade students and several teachers went to the computer university or Unikom for short. When they entered Unikom, we were welcomed by students from Unikom.

seccond ACTIVITY

After being welcomed by UNIKOM students, the Principal of Al Irsyad Al Islamiyyah Informatics Vocational School, Cirebon City, thanked him for his welcome. He emphasized the importance of collaboration between education and universities to produce a superior generation. He also hopes that this visit can inspire students to continue learning and developing in the field of technology. "Hopefully this will be the start of your success," he said, greeted by loud applause.

third ACTIVITY

After the principal and Unikom residents gave their speech, all the students and teachers as well as us Unikom residents took a group photo before taking a group photo. There was a question and answer session, some asked about scholarships, asked about computer science.

last ACTIVITY

After taking photos together, we were taken around Unikom. After walking around, we went to the prayer room inside Unikom. After praying, we went to the Unikom lobby and took another photo together.

Universitas Komputer Indonesia (disingkat UNIKOM) adalah sebuah perguruan tinggi swasta terkemuka yang berada di kota Bandung, Jawa Barat, tepatnya berlokasi di Jl.Dipatiukur No 112-114. Rektornya saat ini dijabat oleh Prof. Dr. Ir. H. Eddy Soeryanto Soegoto, M.T.[1] Sejarah Universitas Komputer Indonesia (UNIKOM) secara resmi berdiri pada hari Selasa, tanggal 8 Agustus 2000 berdasarkan Surat Keputusan Menteri Pendidikan Nasional nomor 126/D/0/2000.
